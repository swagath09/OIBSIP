About Virat Kohli

King of Modern Test Cricket

Virat Kohli is regarded as one of the greatest Test batters of the modern era. His aggressive captaincy, unmatched fitness standards, and hunger for big centuries transformed Indian cricket into one of the strongest Test teams in the world.

His journey from Delhi's domestic circuit to becoming the backbone of India's batting lineup inspired millions of aspiring cricketers worldwide.

Captaincy Legacy

Captaincy Legacy

Virat Kohli captained India in 68 Test matches, winning 40 of them. Under his leadership India became the No.1 ranked Test side and achieved the historic Test series victory in Australia (2018–19).

Historic Test Moments

🏏 Lord's Test 2021

England vs India • Lord's Cricket Ground

On the final day, with only a few overs left, Virat Kohli delivered one of the most iconic team talks in Indian cricket.

"For 60 overs, they should feel hell out there."

Inspired by Kohli's aggressive leadership, Mohammed Siraj, Jasprit Bumrah, Mohammed Shami, and the Indian bowling attack dismissed England and secured one of India's greatest overseas Test victories.
